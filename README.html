ingredients

  • 2chiken breasts
  • 1 onion
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 cup mushrooms
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 tsp dried oregano

instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F
  2. Season chicken breasts with salt, pepper, and oregano
  3. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat
  4. Add onion and garlic, sauté until fragrant
  5. Add mushrooms and broccoli, cook for 5 minutes
  6. Place chicken breasts in a baking dish
  7. Pour the vegetable mixture over the chicken
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes
  9. Serve hot with rice or pasta
